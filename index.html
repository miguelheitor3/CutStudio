How fast is turnaround?+
Your first draft arrives within 48 hours of your editor receiving your footage. Revisions on Growth and Studio plans turn around within 24 hours.
What footage formats do you accept?+
Any format your camera, phone, or screen recorder produces — MP4, MOV, ProRes, and more. We'll let you know if something needs converting before you upload.
How many revisions do I get?+
Starter includes 2 revision rounds per video. Growth and Studio include unlimited revisions until you're happy with the final cut.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. Pause or cancel from your dashboard whenever you need to — your plan simply won't renew.
Who owns the rights to the final video?+
You do, fully. Every video we deliver — and any music or assets we license for it — is yours to use commercially with no restrictions.
What if I only need one video, not a subscription?+
Reach out and we'll put together a custom one-off quote based on length and complexity — no need to commit to a monthly plan.
